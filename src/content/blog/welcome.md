We built Mentric because we believe coaching deserves better tools.

For too long, coaches have relied on a patchwork of spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected apps to manage their practice. Session notes live in one place, goals in another, assessments in a third — and the data that could demonstrate real impact stays locked away, invisible to the people who need it most.

## A platform designed around the coaching relationship

Mentric brings everything together: sessions, goals, assessments, 360 feedback, CPD tracking, messaging, and analytics — all in one beautifully designed platform that puts the coaching relationship at the centre.

Whether you're an independent coach managing a handful of clients or a large organisation running coaching programmes across hundreds of participants, Mentric gives you the structure and visibility to deliver measurable outcomes.

## Measurement that matters

We're particularly passionate about measurement. Not measurement for its own sake, but the kind that helps coaches and coachees see genuine progress — and helps organisations understand the return on their coaching investment.

From repeatable assessments and score trends to 360 feedback and AI-powered insights, Mentric makes it straightforward to track behavioural change over time.

## What's next

This is just the beginning. We'll be sharing more here — practical insights, coaching perspectives, product updates, and conversations with people who are shaping the future of the profession.

Welcome aboard. We're glad you're here.
